A Retrieval-Augmented Generation system for querying mental health treatment protocols with citation-backed answers. Domain-optimized chunking, metadata-rich retrieval, and MMR diversity.

**Key Results:** Precision@5 = 0.78, MRR = 0.91, Factual accuracy 4.3/5.0, Safety 4.8/5.0

**Key Tech:** ChromaDB, MMR retrieval, FastAPI, 500-token chunks with 100-token overlap
